# Cyber Crime Prevention

Umbrella's unified data ingestion and AI-powered analytics provide a robust defense against modern cyber crimes. By correlating data across disparate communication channels and applying advanced behavioral analysis, Umbrella helps organizations detect and prevent sophisticated attacks that traditional, siloed security tools might miss.

## Key Use Cases

### 1. Phishing & Social Engineering Detection
Phishing attacks often involve subtle manipulation and may span multiple platforms (e.g., an initial email followed by a WhatsApp message).
- **Cross-Channel Correlation**: Umbrella links identities across email, Teams, and personal messaging apps, allowing security teams to see the full scope of a phishing campaign.
- **NLP & Sentiment Analysis**: The AI layer analyzes the tone and content of messages to identify common phishing tactics, such as urgent requests for sensitive information or suspicious links, even when they're disguised.

### 2. Business Email Compromise (BEC)
BEC attacks often involve an attacker impersonating a high-level executive to authorize fraudulent wire transfers or disclose sensitive data.
- **Anomaly Detection**: Umbrella establishes a behavioral baseline for users. It can flag unusual communication patterns, such as an executive suddenly contacting the finance department via an unrecorded channel or at unusual hours.
- **Entity Resolution**: By mapping all of a user's communication IDs to a single profile, Umbrella can detect when an external, look-alike account is attempting to impersonate an internal entity.

### 3. Data Leakage & Insider Threat Monitoring
Unauthorized sharing of sensitive information, whether intentional or accidental, poses a significant risk.
- **Lexicon & Pattern Matching**: Umbrella's NLP service can be configured to flag the transmission of sensitive data formats (e.g., credit card numbers, internal project codes) across any monitored channel.
- **Behavioral Profiling**: The Entity Risk Profiler builds a risk score for each entity based on their communication patterns. Sudden spikes in data sharing or communications with high-risk external domains can trigger immediate alerts.

## Leveraging Umbrella's AI Layer

The platform's AI capabilities are central to its effectiveness in cyber crime prevention:
- **Anomaly Detector**: Identifies statistical outliers and behavioral deviations that may indicate a compromised account or an insider threat.
- **Comms Reviewer**: Can be tasked with a first-pass review of flagged "suspicious" communications, providing a reasoned disposition and highlighting the specific evidence of potential cyber crime.
- **Semantic Search**: Allows security analysts to perform natural language queries across all indexed data to proactively hunt for emerging threats or investigate past incidents.
